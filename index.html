Frequently Asked Questions - Your Guide to Cozy Living

How can I contact Cozy Haven customer support?

Our customer support team is here to help. You can reach us through our 'Contact Us' page on the website or email us at support@cozyhaven.com. We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours.

What makes Cozy Haven products special?

Cozy Haven products are crafted with the utmost care and attention to quality. We prioritize comfort, style, and durability to ensure that each piece brings a touch of coziness to your home.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we offer international shipping. Shipping rates and delivery times vary by location. Please check our 'Shipping Information' page for details.

Can I return or exchange a product?

Yes, we want you to be completely satisfied with your Cozy Haven purchase. Our return and exchange policy allows for returns within 30 days of receiving your order. Please refer to our 'Returns and Exchanges' page for more details.

How do I care for my Cozy Haven items?

Care instructions are provided with each product, but in general, we recommend following the labeled care guidelines. Most of our items are machine washable, but delicate items may require special attention. Always check individual product tags for specific care details.
